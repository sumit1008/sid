F. MEX OR Mania

An integer sequence b1,b2,…,bn is good if mex(b1,b2,…,bn)−(b1|b2|…|bn)=1. Here, mex(c) denotes the MEX∗ of the collection c, and | is the bitwise OR operator.
Shohag has an integer sequence a1,a2,…,an. He will perform the following q updates on a:
  - i x — increase ai by x.
After each update, help him find the length of the longest good subarray† of a.
