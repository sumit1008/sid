F. array-value
You have an array of non-negative integers a1,a2,…,an.
The value of a sub-array of length ≥2, a[l,r]=[al,al+1,…,ar] is the minimum value of ai⊕aj such that l≤i<j≤r, where ⊕ is the xor (exclusive-or) operator.
You have to find the k-th smallest value over all sub-arrays of length ≥2.
