B. Kar Salesman
Karel is a salesman in a car dealership. The dealership has n different models of cars. There are ai cars of the i-th model. Karel is an excellent salesperson and can convince customers to buy up to x cars (of Karel's choice), as long as the cars are from different models.
Determine the minimum number of customers Karel has to bring in to sell all the cars.
