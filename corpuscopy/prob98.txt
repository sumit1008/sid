B. Normal Problem
A string consisting of only characters 'p', 'q', and 'w' is painted on a glass window of a store. Ship walks past the store, standing directly in front of the glass window, and observes string ağ‘. Ship then heads inside the store, looks directly at the same glass window, and observes string bğ‘.
Ship gives you string ağ‘. Your job is to find and output bğ‘.
